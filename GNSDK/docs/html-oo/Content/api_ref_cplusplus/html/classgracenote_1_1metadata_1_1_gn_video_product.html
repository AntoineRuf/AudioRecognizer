Detailed Description

A Product refers to the commercial release of a Film, TV Series, or video content. Products contain a unique commercial code such as a UPC (Univeral Product Code), Hinban, or EAN (European Article Number). Products are for the most part released on a physical format, such as a DVD or Blu-ray.
