Accessing Video GDOs from other Video GDOs

The following table shows a subset of the data model for video GDOs and which GDOs can access other GDOs. For a complete list of GDOs and their relationships, see the Data Model in the GNSDK for Desktop C API Reference.

Input GDOs

GDOs Accessible from Input GDOs

Products
  • AV Works

AV Works
  • Contributors
  • Products
  • Seasons
  • Series

AV Work GDOs has the following two optional relationships:

    • Series: Certain AV Work are standalone and are not categorized by Series.
    • Seasons: Some Series are not categorized by Seasons.

Contributors
  • Seasons
  • Series
  • AV Works

Seasons
  • Contributors
  • Series
  • AV Works

Series

  • Contributors
  • Seasons
  • AV Works

Series GDOs can access two types of AV Work GDOs:

  • AV Works associated with (organized by) Seasons
  • AV Works not associated with Seasons GDOs, such as television pilots and specials, and any television series not categorized by Seasons.
